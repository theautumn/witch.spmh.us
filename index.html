Museum

I'm a switch witch at the Connections Museum, in Seattle. I keep the machines running, so that visitors can come and enjoy them. I am particularly proud of this. Most of the older folks have either passed away, or can't come in as much anymore, so someone must take up the task. I am glad to do it!

Music

Music is one of my primary forms of emotional expression. I have been playing for most of my life, however I tend to avoid memorization and theory, and instead play primarily with intuition. I find that some musicians like this, and some don't. Instruments I am reasonably fluent with guitar, bass, drums, sitar, and my voice. I have been writing, but have not released anything in a while. My music can mostly be found here.
